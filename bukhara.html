Inside the Ensemble: Bukhara

Inscribed in 1993 as an "urban ensemble," Bukhara's UNESCO nomination embedded 11 religious buildings within a narrative of medieval urbanism. Mosques and madrasas appear not as standalone pilgrimage sites, but as integral components of fortress walls, trading domes, and public squares — a secular framework inherited from Soviet heritage management.
