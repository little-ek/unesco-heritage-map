Global UNESCO World Heritage Sites

There are 1,247 UNESCO World Heritage Sites that span every continent. "Ensemble sites" represent 43% of all cultural World Heritage Sites (437 of 1,016 sites), yet lack formal definition in UNESCO guidelines.

Post-Soviet states inscribe ensemble nominations at 62.5% — nearly triple Western Europe's 23-29% rate.
